# GAMING TOKEN PLATFORM

## Overview
This document provides an overview of the gaming platform, which incorporates tycoon mechanics and an integrated mining system.

## Tycoon Mechanics
- The game will utilize tycoon mechanics, encouraging players to strategically manage resources and build their empires.

## Mining System
- Players can engage in mining activities to earn in-game resources and OEL tokens.

## OEL Tokens
- OEL tokens are the primary currency within the game, used for trading, building, and other in-game transactions.

## Player Earnings
- Players have the opportunity to earn tokens through gameplay and can convert these into real-world value based on market conditions.

## Creator Compensation
- Content creators associated with the platform will receive compensation based on player engagement and token sales.
